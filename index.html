About Amanda

Quick Facts

  • Licensed in New Jersey & New York
  • Wurzweiler School of Social Work, Class of 1988
  • 36+ years of clinical experience
  • Jackson, NJ 08527
  • In-Person & Telehealth

With over 36 years of dedicated clinical experience, I have had the privilege of helping individuals, couples, and families navigate life's most complex challenges. Since graduating from the Wurzweiler School of Social Work in 1988, I have built my practice on a foundation of warmth, respect, and genuine connection.

My approach begins with creating a space where you feel truly comfortable and heard. From there, we work together to identify the stressors and needs that brought you to therapy, and we build a personalized plan to address them. I believe that everyone is doing their best, and that with the right support, meaningful change is possible.

I take an eclectic approach to therapy, drawing from a range of evidence-based modalities to find what works best for you. Because no two people are alike, I incorporate techniques from Family Systems, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), Hypnotherapy, Psychodynamic Therapy, Strength-Based approaches, and Structural Family Therapy. As I often tell my clients: "It is really about what works to help you the most."

I am passionate about helping people develop their own personal support team and discover creative, healing solutions to the challenges they face. Whether you are working through relationship difficulties, supporting a child with special needs, managing anxiety or depression, or navigating a major life transition, I am here to walk alongside you every step of the way.
