Out-of-Network Benefits

Even if your insurance plan is not listed above, you may still have out-of-network benefits that can help cover the cost of therapy. This means your insurance company may reimburse you for a portion of each session after you pay upfront. I can provide you with a superbill (a detailed receipt) that you can submit to your insurance for reimbursement. Contact your insurance company and ask about your out-of-network mental health benefits to find out what you may be eligible for.

Not Sure If Your Insurance Covers Therapy?

Call the number on the back of your insurance card and ask these questions:

1

“Do I have out-of-network mental health benefits?”

2

“What is my deductible for outpatient mental health?”

3

“What percentage do you reimburse after the deductible?”

4

“Is there a limit on the number of sessions per year?”

Tip: Ask for a reference number for the call. Amanda can provide a superbill (detailed receipt) for you to submit for reimbursement.
