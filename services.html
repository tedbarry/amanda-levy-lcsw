Services & Specialties

Comprehensive therapy tailored to your unique needs, serving preteens through elders — individuals, couples, and families.

Children with Special Needs

Specialized support for families navigating ADHD, autism spectrum, developmental disorders, intellectual disabilities, and learning differences. Helping children thrive and families find strength.

Relationship Issues

Whether you are navigating marital challenges, family conflict, divorce, or premarital planning, therapy provides a safe space to rebuild communication, trust, and connection.

Personality Disorders

Compassionate, informed treatment using DBT, CBT, and psychodynamic approaches to help clients understand patterns, develop coping skills, and build more fulfilling lives.

What to Expect

Your journey toward healing is simpler than you think. Here’s how we get started.

1

Free Phone Consultation

15 minutes • No obligation

We’ll have a brief, friendly chat so you can share what you’re looking for and ask any questions. This helps us both see if we’re a good fit.

2

First Session

50 minutes • Getting to know each other

No pressure — this is about getting comfortable. We’ll talk about what brought you here, identify your stressors, and start building a personalized plan together.

3

Ongoing Sessions

Weekly or as needed • At your pace

We’ll meet regularly to work through challenges, build skills, and track your progress. Sessions are tailored to your unique needs and goals.

4

Growth & Progress

Celebrate your wins

As you develop new coping strategies and insights, you’ll start to see meaningful changes in your relationships, mood, and daily life. We celebrate every step forward.
